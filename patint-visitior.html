Patients and Visitors

Visiting Hours

For patinits:

  • Weekdays: 4:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Weekends: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM

For visitors:

  • Weekdays: 12:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Weekends: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Visitor/ Patient Rules and Regulations

  • Visitors should respect patient privacy and maintain a quiet environment.
  • Visitors must follow health precautions, including hand hygiene and wearing masks in certain areas.
  • Age restrictions may apply, limiting access for young children.
  • Patients are responsible for understanding their treatment plan and asking questions.
  • Patients are encouraged to communicate their needs or concerns to the care team.
  • Patients should inform staff of any visitors.
